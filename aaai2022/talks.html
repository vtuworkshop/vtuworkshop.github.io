Beata Beigman Klebanov

Bio

Dr.Klebanov is a Senior Research Scientist at Educational Testing Service, Princeton, New Jersey.

Dr.Klebanov holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the Hebrew University of Jerusalem, Israel. Before joining ETS, she was a post-doctoral fellow at the Kellogg's School of Management and Northwestern's Institute for Complex Systems where she used computational approaches to analyze political rhetoric.

At ETS, she has been building language technology to encourage developing and sometimes reluctant readers and writers to read and write. She is the principal investigator behind Relay Reader, a reading and listening app, and Writing Mentor, a tool to help writers with academic writing. Relay Reader is listed first in "the best free reading apps for kids" by DigitalTrends and FreeKidsBooks and made the list on ScreenRant and Troomi. It was used for summer programming at the Camden Dream Center, NJ (remote) and NJRISE Youth and Teens program in 2021.
